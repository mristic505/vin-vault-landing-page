OUR WINEMAKER

Chris Willis is an award-winning winemaker and the creative force behind Vin Vault premium wines in a box. Always in search of a new challenge, Chris was drawn to the wine industry for the thrill of having just one chance to craft a new vintage each harvest. With Vin Vault, Chris has pushed himself to craft not only great wine, but wine that actually benefits from the unique qualities of a box.

ABOUT

Vin Vault offers a portfolio of award-winning, premium wines that are specially crafted to benefit from the unique qualities of a box. Each of Vin Vault’s six varietals will stay fresh for up to four weeks after opening and offer the delicious complexity you’d expect from even a great bottle of wine.

OUR CRAFT

From the beginning, Vin Vault was crafted to be different. When others made premium wine and put it in a box, Vin Vault crafted bolder, more robust wines that thrive with the extra oxygen a box provides. This ensures every glass of Vin Vault features the same delicious complexity you’d expect from even a great bottle of wine.
